Three Bears Perks

Indulge in the little conveniences that make a big difference during your stay at our motel. Enjoy complimentary WiFi to stay connected, and rest easy knowing that all of our rooms are designated as non-smoking for your comfort. If you're traveling with a trailer, worry not – we offer convenient off-street parking. We understand that pets are family too, which is why we have pet-friendly rooms ready to welcome your furry companions. Plus, we're pleased to share that there are no extra fees for credit card transactions.

Your comfort is our priority, and our cozy rooms come with the assurance of a dedicated and welcoming team. Your home away from home awaits, where your comfort and joy are our top priorities.

Seasonal Tipi

Sleeping Under the Stars

We're thrilled to offer you a chance to unwind in a roomy tipi while enjoying the embrace of a luxurious queen bed. No need to worry about bedding – we've got that covered for a good night's sleep. Your tipi oasis is thoughtfully equipped with comfy chairs and tables. Step outside and delight in a charming picnic table and, when the stars align with seasonal fire regulations, a soothing evening bonfire. Just a heads up, our charming spot in Lincoln is nestled in Bear Country, so do keep your food stored safely. We can't wait to make your stay special!
