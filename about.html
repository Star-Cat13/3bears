About Three Bears Motel

Three Bears Motel is a family-owned and operated business in the heart of Lincoln, Montana, conveniently located on Highway 200. We take pride in offering a clean, quiet place to stay and a friendly, personal experience for every guest. Whether you're passing through, working in the area, or here to explore the outdoors, we’re here to make your stay comfortable and easy.

Three Bears Perks

The little things that make your stay easier and more comfortable:

  • Complimentary Wi-Fi
  • Non-smoking rooms
  • Off-street parking, including space for trailers
  • Pet-friendly rooms available
  • No extra fees for credit card payments

Our goal is simple: a quiet place to rest and a friendly experience you can rely on.

Seasonal Tipi

Sleeping Under the Stars

Sleep under the stars in a spacious tipi with a queen bed and simple amenities for a relaxing stay. Each tipi includes seating and a small table, with a picnic area just outside. When conditions allow, guests can gather around a seasonal evening bonfire. Lincoln is located in bear country, so all food must be stored securely. We’re excited to share this unique Montana experience with you.

Tipi Guest Info

  • Access to a bathroom and shower house
  • Tipis are not pet-friendly
  • No electricity available at this time

Cancellation Policy

To avoid being charged the full rate of your reservation, please call us at least 2 days prior to your reserved check-in date to cancel.
